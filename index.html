Description:

Campus Lender is a web-based system designed for on-campus students. It enables seamless financial transactions without requiring traditional bank details. Description:

Developed a cloud-based electronic health records system for secure storage, retrieval, and real-time updates of patient medical records. This web portal can be accessed over a private network from various devices, ensuring easy and secure access for both healthcare providers and patients. The system eliminates the need for physical medical records, allowing registered users to access their information seamlessly, enhancing continuity of care and eliminating paper prescriptions.
